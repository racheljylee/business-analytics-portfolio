# Big Data & AI in Business Strategy

This repository contains selected work from the course *Big Data & AI in Business Strategy* (Master in Business Analytics and Data Science, IE University), covering both applied business problem-solving and industry-level analysis.

## Individual Assignment: Impact of Generative AI on Hollywood

### Overview

Analysis of how generative AI is transforming the film and entertainment industry, focusing on both its strategic advantages and structural risks.

### Key Insights

* GenAI is already embedded across production, post-production, and content creation workflows
* Studios are leveraging AI for cost reduction, time efficiency, and enhanced creative capabilities
* At the same time, the industry faces significant challenges around job displacement, intellectual property, and ownership rights

### Strategic Implications

* Shift in competitive dynamics as AI-native capabilities become a differentiator
* Increasing collaboration between technology firms and traditional studios
* Long-term tension between automation and the role of human creativity

### Conclusion

GenAI represents a structural shift rather than a temporary trend, with lasting impact on how content is created, owned, and monetized across the industry.
