## Business Problem

Real estate agencies face:

- Limited visibility into **true asset value**
- Inconsistent pricing across similar properties  
- Missed revenue from underpricing and vacancy risk from overpricing  

**Key question:**  
*Where are the largest pricing gaps—and how can they be captured?*

---

## Approach

### 1. Market Segmentation
- Clustered properties into **4 distinct groups**
- Enabled **like-for-like comparisons** across asset types  
- Focused analysis on the **core market segment (mid-size apartments)**  

### 2. Pricing Model
- Built regression model to estimate **fair rental value**
- Addressed:
  - multicollinearity (VIF)
  - model selection (level vs log)
  - validation (train/test/holdout)

- Achieved **~70% explanatory power (R² ≈ 0.70)**  

---

## Key Insights

- **Market inefficiencies are significant**
  - ~25% underpriced
  - ~20% overpriced  

- **Pricing is structurally driven**
  - Size (primary driver)
  - Location premiums (Salamanca, Centro, Chamberí)
  - Floor level impact  

- **Opportunities are geographically concentrated**
  - Underpricing: Retiro, Centro, Moncloa  
  - Overpricing: Arganzuela, Ciudad Lineal  

---

## Strategic Recommendations

- **Capture upside in underpriced areas**
- **Correct overpricing to reduce vacancy risk**
- **Prioritize high-value segments (large properties in premium districts)**
